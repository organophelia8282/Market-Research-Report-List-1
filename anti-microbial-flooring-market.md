<p><p>The antimicrobial flooring market is categorized primarily into surface coating and mixed markets. Surface coating involves applying antimicrobial agents directly onto flooring materials, enhancing their ability to resist bacteria, mold, and other pathogens. This method provides a protective layer without altering the base material. The mixed market combines various flooring types, integrating antimicrobial properties throughout the material itself, such as in tiles or vinyl. This approach offers comprehensive protection and durability, ideal for high-traffic areas requiring stringent hygiene standards.</p></p>
