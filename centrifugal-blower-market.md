<p><p>A centrifugal blower is a type of mechanical device used to move air or gases through a system by converting rotational energy, typically from an electric motor, into kinetic energy. This is achieved through the use of an impeller that spins at high speeds, creating a pressure difference that draws air into the blower and expels it at high velocity. Centrifugal blowers are widely utilized across various industries, including HVAC, manufacturing, and wastewater treatment, due to their efficiency and reliability.</p><p>The Centrifugal Blower Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.00% during the forecast period, driven by increasing industrialization and growing demand for efficient air management solutions.
