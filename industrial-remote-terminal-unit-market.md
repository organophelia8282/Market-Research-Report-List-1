<p><ul><li>Wireless industrial RTU</li><li>Wired industrial RTU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Remote Terminal Unit (RTU) market is divided into two main types: wireless industrial RTUs and wired industrial RTUs. Wireless industrial RTUs utilize radio frequency or cellular networks for data transmission, offering greater flexibility and easier installation, making them ideal for remote or hard-to-reach locations. In contrast, wired industrial RTUs connect directly to communication lines, providing reliable and secure data transfer, commonly used in environments where stability and uninterrupted connectivity are essential, such as manufacturing plants and utility operations.</p></p>
