<p><p>An automatic gearbox, also known as an automatic transmission, is a type of vehicle transmission system that automatically changes gear ratios as the vehicle moves, eliminating the need for manual gear shifting by the driver. It enhances driving convenience, improves fuel efficiency, and reduces driver fatigue, making it a popular choice in modern vehicles. Automatic gearboxes are available in various types, including torque converter, continuously variable transmission (CVT), and dual-clutch transmission (DCT), each offering unique advantages in terms of performance and efficiency.</p><p>The Automatic Gearbox Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for passenger and commercial vehicles, advancements in transmission technologies, and the rising preference for convenience and comfort in driving.
