<p><p>Square Hollow Section (SHS) steel is widely used in construction and engineering due to its strength and versatility. The market includes two main types: Welded SHS and Seamless SHS. Welded SHS is formed by welding steel plates or coils, offering cost-effectiveness and suitability for structural applications. Seamless SHS is manufactured without welding, providing superior strength and uniformity, ideal for high-pressure or critical applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, with Welded SHS dominating general construction and Seamless SHS preferred for specialized, high-performance needs.</p></p>
