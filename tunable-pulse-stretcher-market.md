<p><strong>The Tunable Pulse Stretcher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fiber Bragg grating Dispersion(FBG)</li><li>Chirped Fiber Bragg Grating(CFBG)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tunable pulse stretcher market includes two primary types: Fiber Bragg Gratings (FBG) and Chirped Fiber Bragg Gratings (CFBG). FBG utilizes periodic variations in refractive index within a fiber to reflect specific wavelengths while allowing others to pass, effectively stretching optical pulses. CFBG, on the other hand, features a gradient in grating period, enabling broader wavelength manipulation and customizable dispersion characteristics. Both technologies enhance performance in optical systems, particularly in telecommunications and high-speed data transmission applications.</p></p>
