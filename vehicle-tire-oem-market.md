<p><p>The Vehicle Tire OEM market primarily consists of Radial and Bias tires. Radial tires feature layers of fabric plies arranged at 90 degrees to the direction of travel, offering better fuel efficiency, durability, and grip, making them popular for modern vehicles. Bias tires have crisscrossed plies at 30-40 degree angles, providing stiffer sidewalls and higher load capacity, often used for heavy-duty or off-road applications. Radial tires dominate the market due to superior performance, while Bias tires remain niche for specific industrial or vintage vehicle needs.</p></p>
