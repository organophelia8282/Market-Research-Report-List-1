<p><strong>The Coffee Pod Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soft Coffee</li><li>Hard Coffee</li><li>Capsules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coffee pod market comprises three primary types: soft coffee pods, hard coffee pods, and capsules. Soft coffee pods are often made with ground coffee enclosed in a filter paper, providing a lighter brew. Hard coffee pods, typically made of plastic or aluminum, offer a sturdier structure and are compatible with specific brewing machines, producing a stronger flavor. Capsules, sealed tightly, contain coffee grounds and sometimes additional ingredients, designed for convenience and consistent brewing quality in single-serve machines.</p></p>
