<p><p>The vacuum hopper loaders market is categorized into two main types: integral and split types. Integral type loaders combine both the hopper and vacuum unit into a single unit, offering a compact design ideal for limited spaces and easy mobility. In contrast, split type loaders feature a separate hopper and vacuum system, allowing for more flexibility and enhanced capacity management. Both types serve the purpose of efficiently transporting bulk materials in various industries, including plastics and pharmaceuticals, ensuring streamlined operations.</p></p>
