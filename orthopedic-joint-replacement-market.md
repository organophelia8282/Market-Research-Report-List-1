<p><strong>The Orthopedic Joint Replacement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Total Replacement</li><li>Partial Replacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orthopedic joint replacement market is primarily divided into two types: total replacement and partial replacement. Total joint replacement involves the complete removal of a damaged joint, such as the hip or knee, and its replacement with an artificial prosthesis, restoring functionality and alleviating pain. In contrast, partial joint replacement focuses on resurfacing only the damaged portion of the joint while preserving healthy surrounding bone and cartilage. Both approaches aim to enhance mobility and improve the quality of life for patients with joint disorders.</p></p>
