<p><p>The Polyvinylidene Chloride (PVDC) coated film market is segmented into two main types: coated PVDC one side and coated PVDC two side. Coated PVDC one side features a protective layer on only one face, making it suitable for applications where barrier properties are essential on one side, such as packaging. Conversely, coated PVDC two side offers enhanced protection by applying the coating on both sides, providing superior moisture, oxygen, and aroma resistance, ideal for more demanding packaging requirements across various industries.</p></p>
