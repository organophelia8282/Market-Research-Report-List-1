<p><p>The Functional Additives Market includes various types such as Antistatic, Antioxidants, Anti-fog, Antimicrobial, UV Stabilizers, and Others. Antistatic additives reduce static electricity in materials, enhancing safety and performance. Antioxidants prevent degradation caused by oxidation, extending product lifespan. Anti-fog additives improve clarity by preventing condensation on surfaces. Antimicrobial additives inhibit microbial growth, ensuring hygiene. UV Stabilizers protect materials from UV radiation, preventing discoloration and degradation. Other additives cater to specific needs like flame retardancy or slip resistance, enhancing material properties for diverse applications.</p></p>
