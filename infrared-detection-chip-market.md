<p><p>The infrared detection chip market is categorized into two main types: uncooled and cooled. Uncooled infrared detectors operate at ambient temperatures, making them cost-effective and compact, suitable for applications like thermal imaging and temperature measurement. In contrast, cooled infrared detectors function at lower temperatures, enhancing sensitivity and performance, ideal for military and space applications. The distinction between the two types lies in thermal management, affecting cost, size, and intended use, with both segments catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
