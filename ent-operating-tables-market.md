<p><ul><li>Electric Operating Tables</li><li>Hydraulic Operating Tables</li><li>Manual Operating Tables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ENT operating tables market comprises three primary types: electric, hydraulic, and manual operating tables. Electric operating tables offer precise positioning and can be adjusted with minimal effort, enhancing efficiency during procedures. Hydraulic operating tables utilize a fluid-based system for height and tilt adjustments, providing stability and flexibility. Manual operating tables rely on mechanical systems, requiring manual effort for adjustments, making them more cost-effective but less convenient. Each type serves specific needs in ENT surgeries, balancing functionality, cost, and ease of use.</p></p>
