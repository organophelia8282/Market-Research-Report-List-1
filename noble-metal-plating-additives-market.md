<p><ul><li>Solid/Powder</li><li>Solution/Concentrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Noble metal plating additives are categorized into two main types: solid/powder and solution/concentrate. The solid/powder form typically consists of dry formulations that can be mixed with plating solutions to enhance the deposition of noble metals like gold and platinum, improving properties such as adhesion and corrosion resistance. Conversely, solution/concentrate additives are liquid formulations, often pre-mixed for immediate use, designed to optimize plating processes through enhanced stability, conductivity, and uniformity, facilitating efficient and high-quality metal deposition.</p></p>
