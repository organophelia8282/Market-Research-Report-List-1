<p><p>The pneumatic ISO cylinders market is categorized mainly into single-acting and double-acting cylinders, along with other types. Single-acting cylinders utilize air pressure for movement in one direction, employing a spring for return, making them ideal for applications with limited space. Double-acting cylinders use air pressure for both extension and retraction, offering greater force and control, suitable for more demanding applications. Other types may include specialty designs for specific industrial needs, enhancing versatility in automation processes.</p></p>
