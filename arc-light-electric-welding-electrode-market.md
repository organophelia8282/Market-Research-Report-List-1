<p><strong>The Arc Light Electric Welding Electrode Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Consumable Welding Electrode</li><li>Non-consumable Welding Electrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Arc Light Electric Welding Electrode market is categorized into two primary types: consumable and non-consumable welding electrodes. Consumable welding electrodes are designed to melt and become part of the weld, providing filler material during the process. They are typically used in processes like MIG and SMAW. In contrast, non-consumable welding electrodes do not melt, serving primarily as a source of heat while maintaining their form, commonly utilized in TIG welding. Each type serves distinct applications and welding techniques.</p></p>
