<p><ul><li>Daily Use</li><li>Operation Use</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical tablets market can be categorized into two main types: daily-use tablets and operation-use tablets. Daily-use tablets are typically prescribed for ongoing health management, such as chronic conditions, prevention, or general wellness, requiring regular consumption for effectiveness. In contrast, operation-use tablets, often referred to as preoperative or postoperative medications, are administered before or after surgical procedures to manage pain, prevent infection, or aid recovery. Both categories play critical roles in patient care and treatment outcomes.</p></p>
