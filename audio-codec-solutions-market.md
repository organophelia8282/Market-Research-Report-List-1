<p><p>The audio codec solutions market is divided into three main types: Mono-codec, Stereo-codec, and Multi-channel codec. Mono-codecs handle single-channel audio, ideal for basic voice communication and low-bandwidth applications. Stereo-codecs support two channels, enhancing audio quality for music and multimedia. Multi-channel codecs enable more complex sound experiences, accommodating multiple audio channels for surround sound in home theaters and gaming. Each type caters to different needs, balancing quality, bandwidth, and application requirements in the audio landscape.</p></p>
