<p><ul><li>Free Cooling</li><li>Absorption Cooling</li><li>Compression Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The District Cooling Energy System market encompasses three primary types: Free Cooling, Absorption Cooling, and Compression Cooling. Free Cooling utilizes ambient air or water to lower temperatures, minimizing energy consumption. Absorption Cooling relies on heat sources, such as natural gas or waste heat, to drive a cooling process, making it efficient in energy use. Compression Cooling, the most common method, employs mechanical compressors to achieve chilling, offering reliability and versatility. Together, these technologies enhance energy efficiency and sustainability in district cooling systems.</p></p>
