<p><p>The artificial barrier casings market includes various types designed for food processing. Collagen casings are derived from animal collagen and are used for sausages. Cellulose casings are plant-based, commonly used for smokehouse applications. Plastic casings provide moisture and oxygen barriers, ideal for vacuum-sealed products. Fibrous casings are made from cellulose and offer strength for larger sausages. Value-added casings include features like flavor infusion, while textile casings are typically used for traditional meats. Net casings provide a distinct visual appeal and are often used for specialty products.</p></p>
