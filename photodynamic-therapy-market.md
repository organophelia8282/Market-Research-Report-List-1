<p><p>Photodynamic Therapy (PDT) is a medical treatment that uses photosensitizing agents combined with light to destroy cancerous or pathological cells. When photosensitizing drugs are exposed to specific wavelengths of light, they produce reactive oxygen species that selectively target and kill diseased cells while minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissue. This innovative treatment is gaining traction for its applications in oncology, dermatology, and eye diseases.</p><p>The Photodynamic Therapy Market is poised for growth, expected to expand at a CAGR of 4.00% during the forecast period. <p><ul><li>Laser Therapy Devices</li><li>Photosensitizer Drugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photodynamic therapy (PDT) market encompasses two main types: laser therapy devices and photosensitizer drugs. Laser therapy devices deliver focused light to activate photosensitizers, which are compounds that react with light to produce reactive oxygen species, targeting and destroying cancer cells or harmful tissues. Photosensitizer drugs are essential components that absorb light and initiate the therapeutic effect during PDT. Together, these elements offer a minimally invasive treatment option for various conditions, including cancer and skin disorders, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
