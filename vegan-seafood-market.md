<p><p>The vegan seafood market encompasses various product types, appealing to diverse consumer preferences. Fish sticks are typically made from plant-based ingredients, mimicking the texture and flavor of traditional fish. Tuna substitutes, crafted from ingredients like chickpeas or seaweed, aim to recreate tuna's flavor.
