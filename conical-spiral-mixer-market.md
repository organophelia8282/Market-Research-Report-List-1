<p><p>The conical spiral mixer market is divided into two main types: continuous and discontinuous. Continuous conical spiral mixers operate in a nonstop manner, allowing for efficient and high-capacity mixing processes, ideal for large-scale production. In contrast, discontinuous mixers work in batches, facilitating flexibility and precise control over the mixing process, making them suitable for smaller volumes or specialized applications. Both types cater to different production needs, reflecting the diverse requirements of the food, pharmaceutical, and chemical industries.</p></p>
