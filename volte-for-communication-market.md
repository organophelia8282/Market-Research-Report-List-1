<p><strong>The VoLTE for Communication Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voice Over IP Multimedia Subsystem (VoIMS)</li><li>Circuit Switched Fall Back (CSFB)</li><li>Dual Radio/Simultaneous Voice and LTE (SVLTE)</li><li>Voice Over LTE Via Generic Access Network (VOLGA)</li><li>Single Radio Voice Call Continuity (SRVCC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VoLTE (Voice over LTE) enables high-quality voice calls over 4G networks using IP Multimedia Subsystem (IMS). VoIMS leverages IMS for seamless voice and multimedia services. CSFB (Circuit Switched Fall Back) allows LTE devices to switch to 2G/3G for voice calls. SVLTE (Simultaneous Voice and LTE) supports concurrent voice and data using dual radios. VOLGA (Voice over LTE via Generic Access Network) extends VoLTE over Wi-Fi. SRVCC (Single Radio Voice Call Continuity) ensures smooth handover of VoLTE calls to 2G/3G networks, maintaining call continuity. These technologies cater to diverse market needs for voice and data integration.</p></p>
