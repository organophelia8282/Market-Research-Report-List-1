<p><strong>The Fully Automatic Vacuum Packaging Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>V-FFS</li><li>H-FFS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fully automatic vacuum packaging machine market is primarily categorized into two types: Vertical Form-Fill-Seal (V-FFS) and Horizontal Form-Fill-Seal (H-FFS) machines. V-FFS machines create bags from a flat roll of film, filling and sealing them vertically, making them ideal for loose products like powders and granules. In contrast, H-FFS machines operate horizontally, suitable for solid products like meats and pastries. Both systems enhance packaging efficiency, extend shelf life, and maintain product integrity, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
