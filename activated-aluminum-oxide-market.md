<p><p>The activated aluminum oxide market consists of two primary types: activated aluminum oxide powders and activated aluminum oxide spheres. Activated aluminum oxide powders are fine particles used in various applications such as catalysts, adsorbents, and drying agents due to their high surface area and porosity. Activated aluminum oxide spheres, on the other hand, are spherical granules often utilized in specialized filtration systems, chromatography, and as catalyst supports, offering uniformity and improved flow characteristics in industrial processes.</p></p>
