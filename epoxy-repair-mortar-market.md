<p><p>The epoxy repair mortar market encompasses two primary types: two-component epoxy mortar and three-component epoxy mortar. Two-component epoxy mortar consists of a resin and a hardener, ensuring a strong bond and high durability. It is commonly used for repairing concrete surfaces, offering resistance to chemicals and weathering. Three-component epoxy mortar includes an additional component, often a filler or aggregate, enhancing its strength and workability. This variant is ideal for heavy-duty applications, providing superior performance in demanding environments.</p></p>
