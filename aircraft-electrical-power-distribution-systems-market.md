<p><ul><li>PEPDS (Primary Electrical Power Distribution System)</li><li>SPDS (Secondary Power Distribution System)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Electrical Power Distribution Systems market is categorized into two main types: Primary Electrical Power Distribution System (PEPDS) and Secondary Power Distribution System (SPDS). PEPDS focuses on the main generation and distribution of electrical power throughout the aircraft, ensuring essential systems operate efficiently. In contrast, SPDS handles the distribution of power to secondary systems, enabling auxiliary functions like lighting and entertainment. Together, these systems ensure reliable and efficient electrical power management across various aircraft types.</p></p>
