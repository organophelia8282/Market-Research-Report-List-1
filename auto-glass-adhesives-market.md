<p><p>The auto glass adhesives market primarily consists of silicone and polyurethane adhesives, each offering distinct advantages. Silicone adhesives provide flexibility and resistance to temperature fluctuations, making them suitable for applications requiring movement and sealant properties. In contrast, polyurethane adhesives are known for their superior bonding strength and durability, often preferred for structural applications where safety is paramount. Both types play critical roles in ensuring secure and effective installation of automotive glass, catering to different needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
