<p><p>The coating pigments market is categorized into organic and inorganic types. Organic coating pigments are carbon-based compounds known for their vibrant colors and ease of dispersion in various media, making them ideal for applications in paints, coatings, and plastics. In contrast, inorganic coating pigments are derived from mineral sources and offer excellent durability, lightfastness, and opacity. These pigments are commonly used in industrial applications, providing stability under harsh conditions and enhancing the longevity of coatings.</p></p>
