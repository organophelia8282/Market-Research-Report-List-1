<p><strong>The Military Phased Array Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AESA</li><li>PESA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military phased array antenna market primarily comprises two types: Active Electronically Scanned Array (AESA) and Passive Electronically Scanned Array (PESA) systems. AESA antennas utilize multiple transmit/receive modules, allowing for rapid beam steering and enhanced performance in diverse communication and radar applications. They offer superior flexibility and resilience. In contrast, PESA antennas use a single transmit/receive module to distribute power across elements, making them simpler but less adaptable. Both types play crucial roles in modern military operations, emphasizing detection, tracking, and communication capabilities.</p></p>
