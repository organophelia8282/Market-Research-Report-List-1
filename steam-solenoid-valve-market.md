<p><ul><li>Saturated Steam Solenoid Valves</li><li>Superheated Steam Solenoid Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam solenoid valve market is primarily categorized into two types: saturated steam solenoid valves and superheated steam solenoid valves. Saturated steam solenoid valves are designed to handle steam at its boiling point, ensuring efficient flow control in processes like heating and sterilization. In contrast, superheated steam solenoid valves manage steam that exceeds its boiling point, suitable for applications requiring higher temperatures and greater energy transfer. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, ensuring optimal performance in steam management systems.</p></p>
