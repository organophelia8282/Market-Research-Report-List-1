<p><p>A Phase Frequency Detector (PFD) is an essential electronic component used in phase-locked loops (PLLs) to compare the phase and frequency of two input signals. It accurately detects phase differences and generates an output signal that drives a voltage-controlled oscillator (VCO) to synchronize the output frequency with the reference frequency. PFDs play a critical role in various applications, including telecommunications, data converters, and frequency synthesizers.</p><p>The Phase Frequency Detector Market is anticipated to experience significant growth, projected to grow at a CAGR of 7.8% during the forecast period.
