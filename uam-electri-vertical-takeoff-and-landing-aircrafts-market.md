<p><p>Urban Air Mobility (UAM) Electric Vertical Takeoff and Landing (eVTOL) aircraft are revolutionizing urban transportation. The market is divided into **Vector Propulsion** and **Vectorless Propulsion** systems. Vector propulsion uses tilting rotors or engines to direct thrust for vertical and horizontal flight, offering precise control and efficiency. Vectorless propulsion relies on fixed rotors or ducted fans, simplifying design but requiring more energy for transitions. Both systems aim to enable quiet, emission-free urban air travel, with vector propulsion excelling in maneuverability and vectorless in simplicity and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
