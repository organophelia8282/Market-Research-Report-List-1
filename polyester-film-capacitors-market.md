<p><strong>The Polyester Film Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plain Polyester Film Capacitors</li><li>Metallised Polyester Film Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyester film capacitors market consists of two primary types: plain polyester film capacitors and metallised polyester film capacitors. Plain polyester film capacitors utilize a non-metalized polyester film, offering basic dielectric properties suitable for applications requiring stability and reliability. In contrast, metallised polyester film capacitors feature a thin metal layer deposited on the film, enhancing capacitance and energy storage capacity while reducing size. Both types are widely used in electronic devices, power electronics, and filtering applications due to their durability and performance attributes.</p></p>
