<p><p>Energy Recovery Units (ERUs) are categorized into three main market types: ceiling-mount, wall-mount, and cabinet-mount. Ceiling-mount ERUs are installed in suspended ceilings, efficiently utilizing overhead space, ideal for commercial settings. Wall-mount ERUs are compact units attached to walls, suitable for residential and small commercial applications where floor space is limited. Cabinet-mount ERUs are enclosed in cabinets, offering a discreet installation option while providing efficient energy recovery for various building sizes, ensuring flexibility and efficient use of space.</p></p>
