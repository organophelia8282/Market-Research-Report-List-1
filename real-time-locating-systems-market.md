<p><p>The Real-Time Locating Systems (RTLS) market encompasses various technologies, each suited for specific applications. RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) offers cost-effective tracking over short distances, ideal for inventory management. Wi-Fi RTLS leverages existing network infrastructure for real-time asset tracking within buildings. UWB (Ultra-Wideband) provides high precision in location accuracy, making it suitable for environments requiring fine-tuned tracking. GPS (Global Positioning System) offers wide-area location tracking, primarily used outdoors, for applications such as fleet management and outdoor asset tracking.</p></p>
