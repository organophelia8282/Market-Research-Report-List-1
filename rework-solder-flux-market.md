<p><p>The rework solder flux market is categorized into three main types based on their activation levels and formulation. Rosin flux, derived from natural pine resin, offers good thermal stability and reduces oxidation during soldering. Rosin mildly activated flux includes added components that enhance solderability while maintaining minimal activation, making it suitable for sensitive electronics. Rosin activated flux contains stronger activators, effectively cleaning surfaces and promoting adhesion, ideal for less pristine environments. Each type serves specific soldering needs in electronics manufacturing and repair.</p></p>
