<p><p>The handheld paint sprayer market is categorized into three primary types: Airless, High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP), and Others. Airless sprayers utilize a pump to force paint through a hose, providing a quickly applied, even finish, ideal for larger projects. HVLP sprayers operate by using a high volume of air at low pressure, resulting in minimal overspray and more controlled application, suitable for intricate detailing. The "Others" category encompasses various specialized sprayers designed for niche applications or unique formulations.</p></p>
