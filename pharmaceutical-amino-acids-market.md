<p><p>The pharmaceutical amino acids market is divided into two main types: essential and non-essential amino acids. Essential amino acids cannot be synthesized by the body and must be obtained through diet; they play crucial roles in protein synthesis and overall health. Non-essential amino acids, on the other hand, can be produced by the body and contribute to various physiological processes, including metabolism and neurotransmitter function. Both types are vital for therapeutic applications in nutrition and medical treatments.</p></p>
