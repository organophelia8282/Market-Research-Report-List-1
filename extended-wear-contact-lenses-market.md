<p><strong>The Extended Wear Contact Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spherical Form</li><li>Toric Form</li><li>Multifocal Form</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The extended wear contact lenses market comprises three primary types: spherical, toric, and multifocal forms. Spherical lenses correct nearsightedness or farsightedness by providing a uniform optical power. Toric lenses are designed for astigmatism, featuring different curvatures to accommodate varying vision needs. Multifocal lenses cater to presbyopia, integrating multiple lens powers for clear vision at different distances. This variety enhances comfort and convenience for users, allowing for prolonged wear and catering to diverse vision correction requirements.</p></p>
