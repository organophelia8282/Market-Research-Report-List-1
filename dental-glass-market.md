<p><ul><li>Inert Glass</li><li>Reactive Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental glass market is categorized into two main types: inert glass and reactive glass. Inert glass is chemically stable and not prone to reaction with saliva or oral tissues, making it ideal for protective barriers and restorative applications. Conversely, reactive glass participates in chemical bonding with dental materials, enhancing adhesion and durability in restorations. Each type serves distinct purposes in dentistry, contributing to improved patient outcomes and the longevity of dental treatments.</p></p>
