<p><ul><li>Bucket Elevators</li><li>Conveyors</li><li>Augers</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Grain handling systems encompass various types of equipment used for transporting and storing grain. Bucket elevators are vertical conveyors consisting of buckets attached to a belt or chain, ideal for moving grain to elevated storage. Conveyors use belts or rollers to transport grain horizontally or at an incline, providing versatility in movement. Augers utilize a helical screw to move grain effectively, suited for loading and unloading. Other systems may include railcars, air systems, and integrated technologies that enhance efficiency in grain handling operations.</p></p>
