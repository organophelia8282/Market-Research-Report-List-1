<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Uninterrupted Power Supply (UPS) Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-line UPS System</li><li>Standby UPS System</li><li>On-line Interactive UPS System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Uninterrupted Power Supply (UPS) systems market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **On-line UPS System** provides continuous power and protects against all disturbances by employing double conversion technology, delivering a stable output. </p><p>2. **Standby UPS System** remains inactive until a power outage, swiftly switching to battery during interruptions, making it cost-effective for less critical applications. </p><p>3. **On-line Interactive UPS System** combines features of both, offering battery backup while regulating voltage fluctuations, serving as a bridge between standby and on-line systems.</p></p>
