<p><p>The rubber anti-viscosity agent market includes several key types: stearates, fatty acid esters, and fatty acid amides. Stearates, often used for their lubricating properties, help reduce friction and improve processing. Fatty acid esters serve as plasticizers, enhancing flexibility and flow during manufacturing. Fatty acid amides provide thermal stability and adhesion properties, often used in rubber formulations to prevent viscosity increase at elevated temperatures. Collectively, these agents optimize rubber processing and enhance performance in various applications.</p></p>
