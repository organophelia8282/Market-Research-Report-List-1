<p><strong>The Pre-Engineered Fire Suppression Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Chemistry Fire Suppression Systems</li><li>Wet Chemistry Fire Suppression Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pre-engineered fire suppression systems are designed for quick installation and effective response to specific fire hazards. Dry chemistry fire suppression systems utilize dry chemicals, such as sodium bicarbonate or potassium carbonate, to extinguish fires, making them ideal for flammable liquid hazards and electrical equipment. In contrast, wet chemistry fire suppression systems use a liquid agent, often a solution of water and chemicals like potassium acetate, to combat fires involving cooking oils or fats, ensuring efficient suppression in commercial kitchens.</p></p>
