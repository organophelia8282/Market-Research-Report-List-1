<p><strong>The Optical Image Stabilizer (OIS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lens Shift Method</li><li>Module Tilt Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Image Stabilizer (OIS) enhances image quality by reducing blurriness caused by camera shake. The Lens Shift Method involves physically shifting the lens elements to counteract movement, making it effective for still photography and video. The Module Tilt Method, on the other hand, tilts the image sensor or lens module, adjusting for motion to stabilize the captured image. Both methods cater to different market segments, with applications in smartphones, digital cameras, and camcorders, improving user experience in capturing high-quality images.</p></p>
