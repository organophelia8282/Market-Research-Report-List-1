<p><strong>The LTE Femto Base Station Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Time Division Duplex (TDD)</li><li>Frequency Division Duplex (FDD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LTE femto base station market is primarily classified into two types: Time Division Duplex (TDD) and Frequency Division Duplex (FDD). TDD utilizes the same frequency channel for both uplink and downlink, alternating between sending and receiving data, which optimizes spectrum usage but can lead to latency issues. Conversely, FDD employs separate frequency bands for uplink and downlink, allowing simultaneous communication, resulting in more stable performance. Each type caters to different network demands and user requirements in dense urban environments.</p></p>
