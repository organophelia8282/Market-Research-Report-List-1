<p><p>The PAT testing equipment market consists of several key types. Fail/Pass PAT Testing ensures devices meet safety standards, marking them as compliant or requiring further action. Earth bond Testing assesses the integrity of the earth connection in appliances. Insulation Testing evaluates the resistance of insulation materials to prevent electrical hazards. Battery Powered Testing focuses on portable devices, ensuring their safety without needing an external power source. Leakage/load Testing checks for current leakage and device performance under load conditions, ensuring overall safety and reliability.</p></p>
