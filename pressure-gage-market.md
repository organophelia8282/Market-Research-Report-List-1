<p><p>The pressure gauge market can be categorized into three main types: absolute pressure gauges, gauge pressure gauges, and vacuum gauges. Absolute pressure gauges measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, providing precise readings essential in scientific applications. Gauge pressure gauges measure pressure relative to ambient atmospheric pressure, commonly used in industrial and automotive settings. Vacuum gauges specifically measure pressures below atmospheric levels, crucial for applications in laboratories and manufacturing processes. Each type serves distinct purposes across various industries, ensuring accurate pressure monitoring.</p></p>
