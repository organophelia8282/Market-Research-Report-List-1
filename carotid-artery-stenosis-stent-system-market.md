<p><p>The Carotid Artery Stenosis Stent System market includes various types of stents designed to treat narrowed carotid arteries. Bare Metal Brackets are basic stents providing structural support without drug delivery. Stent Grafts combine stenting with a graft to reinforce the artery wall. Drug-coated Stents are infused with medication to prevent restenosis, enhancing long-term efficacy. Degradable Metal Vascular Stents are temporary structures that dissolve over time, promoting natural healing while reducing long-term complications associated with permanent implants.</p></p>
