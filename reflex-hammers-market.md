<p><p>The reflex hammer market comprises various types designed for neurological assessment. Babinski and Rabiner hammers feature a rubber head and are used for testing plantar reflexes. Tromner hammers offer a triangular design for more precise reflex elicitation. Dejerine style hammers include an integrated pin for additional testing. Buck style hammers are compact and versatile. Pediatric hammers are designed for children, often with playful designs. Taylor design hammers feature a lightweight and ergonomic construction, while Queen Square and Vernon types offer specialized features for refined assessments. Miscellaneous styles encompass various unique designs catering to specific clinical needs.</p></p>
