<p><p>The COB ceramic substrate market is primarily divided into two types: Al2O3 (alumina) COB ceramic substrates and AlN (aluminum nitride) COB ceramic substrates. Al2O3 substrates are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and good thermal conductivity, making them suitable for various electronic applications. In contrast, AlN substrates offer superior thermal conductivity and electrical insulation, making them ideal for high-performance applications, particularly in power electronics and LED technology. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing device efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
