<p><strong>The Insulated Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>XLPE Insulated Cable</li><li>PVC Insulated Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The insulated cable market primarily features two types: XLPE (Cross-Linked Polyethylene) insulated cables and PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) insulated cables. XLPE cables are known for their superior thermal resistance, making them suitable for high-voltage applications, offering durability and chemical resistance. PVC insulated cables, on the other hand, are widely used in low to medium voltage applications due to their flexibility, cost-effectiveness, and ease of installation. Both types serve distinct requirements across various industries, ensuring safe and efficient electrical transmission.</p></p>
