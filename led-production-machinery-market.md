<p><p>The LED production machinery market encompasses various equipment essential for manufacturing LEDs. MOCVD (Metal-Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment is crucial for creating semiconductor layers. Lithography equipment defines patterns on substrates, while dry etch equipment removes unwanted materials selectively. PECVD (Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment deposits thin films through plasma processes, and PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) equipment facilitates thin film coatings. Back-end LED production equipment focuses on assembling and testing finished LED products, ensuring quality and performance.</p></p>
