<p><p>Pneumatic compression devices for lymphedema include pneumatic compression pumps and pneumatic compression garments. Pneumatic compression pumps are motorized systems that deliver controlled pressure to the affected limb, promoting lymphatic fluid movement and reducing swelling. In contrast, pneumatic compression garments are wearable devices that incorporate air chambers to apply graduated compression, enhancing circulation and lymph drainage. Both types are essential in managing lymphedema by alleviating symptoms, improving mobility, and preventing complications associated with fluid retention.</p></p>
