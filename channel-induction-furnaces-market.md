<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Channel induction furnaces are categorized into horizontal and vertical types based on their design and operational orientation. Horizontal channel induction furnaces allow for easier material loading and unloading, making them ideal for continuous operations and larger batches. In contrast, vertical channel induction furnaces often occupy less floor space and are suitable for melting smaller quantities or specific alloys. Both types utilize electromagnetic induction to heat and melt metal efficiently, catering to different manufacturing needs in the foundry and metallurgical industries.</p></p>
