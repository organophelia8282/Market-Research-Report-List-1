<p><strong>The Implant Borne Prosthetics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bone Level Prosthetics</li><li>Tissue Level Prosthetics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The implant-borne prosthetics market can be categorized into two main types: bone-level prosthetics and tissue-level prosthetics. Bone-level prosthetics are anchored directly into the jawbone, facilitating a stable foundation for dental restorations, often resulting in enhanced aesthetics and function. In contrast, tissue-level prosthetics are designed to sit above the bone, typically integrating with the soft tissue, which simplifies surgical procedures. Both types aim to restore dental function and appearance, catering to diverse patient needs and anatomical conditions.</p></p>
