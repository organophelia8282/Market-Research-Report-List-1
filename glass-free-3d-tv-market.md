<p><p>The Glass Free 3D TV market encompasses two primary types: Active 3D TV and Passive 3D TV. Active 3D TVs utilize liquid crystal shutter technology to deliver depth perception, requiring battery-powered glasses that sync with the display for dynamic viewing. In contrast, Passive 3D TVs use polarized lenses, allowing viewers to experience 3D effects without batteries, making them more comfortable for extended viewing. Both technologies aim to enhance user experience, catering to different preferences in immersive viewing.</p></p>
