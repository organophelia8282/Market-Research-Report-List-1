<p><p>Induction heat treatment services encompass various processes that utilize electromagnetic induction to enhance material properties. Induction hardening increases hardness and fatigue resistance by rapidly heating and cooling metal surfaces. Induction annealing relieves internal stresses and improves ductility by gently heating materials. Induction tempering follows hardening to reduce brittleness and optimize toughness. Other processes may include induction brazing and melting, further expanding applications across industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, ensuring improved performance and longevity of components.</p></p>
