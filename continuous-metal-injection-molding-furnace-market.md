<p><p>The Continuous Metal Injection Molding (CMIM) Furnace is an advanced manufacturing equipment designed for the thermal processing of metal powders used in metal injection molding. CMIM furnaces streamline the debinding and sintering processes, significantly enhancing production speed and reducing costs.</p><p>The Continuous Metal Injection Molding Furnace Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.3% during the forecast period, driven by an increasing demand for lightweight and high-performance components in sectors such as automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics.
