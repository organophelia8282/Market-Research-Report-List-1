<p><p>The construction waterproofing system market encompasses various types aimed at preventing water ingress in structures. Liquid waterproofing membranes are flexible coatings applied to surfaces, forming a seamless barrier. Cementitious waterproofing uses a mixture of cement and additives for robust protection, ideal for internal areas. Bituminous waterproofing, derived from asphalt, provides exceptional durability for roofs and foundations. Lastly, polyurethane waterproofing offers strong adhesion and elasticity, making it suitable for areas with movement, effectively safeguarding structures from water damage.</p></p>
