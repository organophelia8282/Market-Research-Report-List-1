<p><p>The plastic sheet thermoforming machine market is primarily divided into two types: single station and multi-station thermoforming machines. Single station thermoforming machines focus on efficiency and simplicity, handling one production cycle at a time, which is ideal for smaller production runs and custom applications. In contrast, multi-station thermoforming machines optimize productivity by automating multiple stages of the process, enabling higher output and reduced cycle times, making them suitable for large-scale manufacturing operations requiring consistent, high-volume production.</p></p>
