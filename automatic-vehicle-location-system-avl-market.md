<p><p>The Automatic Vehicle Location (AVL) system tracks vehicles in real-time using technologies like Radio Navigation, GPS-based systems, and other methods. **Radio Navigation** relies on radio signals from fixed stations to determine vehicle position, suitable for areas with limited satellite coverage. **GPS-based** systems use satellite signals for precise, global tracking, widely adopted for accuracy and reliability. **Other markets** include hybrid or emerging technologies like cellular networks or inertial navigation, offering alternatives where traditional methods face limitations. Each type caters to specific operational needs and environments.</p></p>
