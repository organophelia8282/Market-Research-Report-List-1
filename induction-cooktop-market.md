<p><p>The induction cooktop market can be categorized into two main types: touch screen and touchtone. Touch screen induction cooktops feature a flat glass surface that allows users to control settings with a fingertip, providing a sleek design and intuitive operation. In contrast, touchtone induction cooktops use physical buttons or knobs for adjustments, offering a more tactile experience. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with touch screens often appealing to tech-savvy users, while touchtone models attract those who prefer simplicity and familiarity.</p></p>
