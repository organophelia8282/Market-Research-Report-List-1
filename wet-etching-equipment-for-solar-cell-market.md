<p><p>Wet etching equipment for the solar cell market involves processes that remove material from the surface of silicon wafers to enhance cell efficiency. Alkaline etching uses basic solutions, typically involving sodium hydroxide, to remove silicon oxide and impurities, promoting better light absorption. Acidic etching employs acidic solutions, such as hydrochloric or nitric acid, to clean the wafer surfaces and create a smoother texture. Both methods are essential for optimizing solar cell performance by enhancing surface properties and reducing reflection.</p></p>
