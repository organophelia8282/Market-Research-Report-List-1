<p><p>The lymphoma molecular diagnostics market encompasses various technologies to analyze genetic alterations in lymphoma. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies specific DNA sequences to detect mutations. FISH (Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization) uses fluorescent probes to visualize chromosomal abnormalities. DNA sequencing determines the precise order of nucleotides in DNA to identify mutations and hereditary risks. Gene chips or microarrays analyze thousands of genes simultaneously, enabling comprehensive gene expression profiling. Together, these tools enhance diagnosis and treatment strategies for lymphoma patients.</p></p>
