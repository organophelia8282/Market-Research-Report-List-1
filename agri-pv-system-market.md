<p><p>Agri-PV systems integrate agriculture with photovoltaic technology, creating two primary market types: ground systems and aquatic systems. Ground systems utilize land for both crop cultivation and solar energy generation, optimizing land use while enhancing agricultural productivity. In contrast, aquatic systems are installed on water bodies, such as lakes and reservoirs, minimizing land competition and reducing water evaporation. Both systems promote sustainable practices, support renewable energy generation, and contribute to food security by effectively combining agriculture and solar power.</p></p>
