<p><p>The truck clutch assembly market includes several types based on transmission systems. </p><p>1. **Manual Transmission (MT)** relies on a driver-operated clutch for gear changes.</p><p>2. **Automated Manual Transmission (AMT)** automates gear shifting while retaining a manual clutch for efficiency.</p><p>3. **Automatic Transmission (AT)** uses a torque converter and does not require driver intervention for gear changes.</p><p>4. **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)** provides seamless shifting with variable gear ratios for optimal performance. </p><p>5. **Other** encompasses alternative or specialized systems geared for specific applications.</p></p>
