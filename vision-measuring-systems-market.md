<p><p>Vision measuring systems can be categorized into two primary types: 2D dimension measurement and 3D dimension measurement. </p><p>2D dimension measurement focuses on capturing and analyzing flat images to assess length, width, and other surface attributes, making it suitable for applications like optical inspections. In contrast, 3D dimension measurement utilizes advanced imaging technologies to capture depth information, enabling precise evaluation of complex geometries and surface profiles. Both types are vital in manufacturing, quality control, and automation for ensuring product accuracy and consistency.</p></p>
