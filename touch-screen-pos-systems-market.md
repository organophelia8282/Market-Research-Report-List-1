<p><p>Touch screen POS systems can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based POS systems operate online, allowing users to access their data and manage transactions from any device with internet connectivity. This model offers flexibility and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premises POS systems are locally installed on hardware within the business, providing direct control over operations and data security. Both types cater to different business needs, influencing factors like cost, scalability, and maintenance.</p></p>
